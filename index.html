Work Experience

Workday

Founded in 2005 by David Duffield, former CEO of PeopleSoft, alongside Aneel Bhusri, former PeopleSoft chief strategist, Workday is a pioneer in HR software as a service (SaaS) and is headquartered in Pleasanton, California. Workday is a provider of cloud-based software that specializes in applications for financial management, enterprise resource planning (ERP) and human capital management (HCM).

I was a Application Intern for 6 months with Workday as part of my course. I gained proficiency in working with Workdays proprietary object-oriented programming language, Xpresso. I also gained proficiency in working with Workdays proprietary automation testing tool, WATS. Finally, I gained proficiency in working with JIRA and improved productivity with Agile project management.

I fulfilled the role of code reviewer, code reviewing the work of more experienced and junior developers. I developed critical thinking, problem solving and adaptability by continuously learning Workdays proprietary systems, Xpresso and development pipeline. Furthermore I gained a concrete understanding of developing Software as a Service (SaaS).
