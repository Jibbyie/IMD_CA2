Hello, my name is Jibril Abdulazeez

I am a 20 year old student from Dublin. I am currently attending TU Dublin Tallaght Campus. I am studying Computer Science with Software Development and I am in my final year.
This four year course gives a great grounding in the essentials of computing leading to rewarding careers. A six-month long internship in Year 3 is part of the course and gives you a real advantage giving you industry experience and contacts. With around one hundred students placed annually across Computing courses in Tallaght the list of placement companies is long and includes major companies such as Amazon AWS, SAP, fidelity & Bank of America as well as Tallaght University Hospital, Evros and many others.
